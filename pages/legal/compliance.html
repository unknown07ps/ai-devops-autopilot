Our Approach to Compliance

Security and compliance are foundational to how we build and operate Deployr. We are actively working toward formal certifications while maintaining the highest security standards in our daily operations.

Our current security measures include:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and audit logging
  • Incident response procedures
  • Employee security training

For compliance inquiries, contact compliance@deployr.com
