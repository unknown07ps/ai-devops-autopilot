Responsible Disclosure

Security vulnerability reporting program

Our Commitment

We take the security of our platform seriously. We appreciate the work of security researchers who help us keep our users safe. If you discover a security vulnerability, we encourage you to report it responsibly.

Report a Vulnerability

Email: security@deployr.com

Scope

The following systems are in scope for security research:

  • Deployr Dashboard (*.deployr.com)
  • Deployr API endpoints
  • Open source components on GitHub

Out of Scope

  • Social engineering attacks
  • Physical security attacks
  • Denial of service attacks
  • Automated scanning without coordination
  • Third-party services we use

What to Include in Your Report

  • Description of the vulnerability
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Potential impact
  • Any proof-of-concept code
  • Your contact information

Our Promise

  • We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
  • We will provide an initial assessment within 72 hours
  • We will keep you informed of our progress
  • We will not pursue legal action against researchers acting in good faith
  • We will credit you (if desired) when the vulnerability is fixed

Safe Harbor

We consider security research conducted in accordance with this policy to be authorized. We will not pursue civil or criminal action against researchers who:

  • Make a good faith effort to avoid privacy violations
  • Do not exploit vulnerabilities beyond what is necessary to demonstrate them
  • Do not access, modify, or delete data belonging to other users
  • Report vulnerabilities promptly and do not disclose publicly before a fix is available
