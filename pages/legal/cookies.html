Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for authentication and security Session
Preferences Remember your settings (theme, timezone) 1 year
Analytics Understand how you use our dashboard 30 days

3. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly. They include cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Security tokens (CSRF protection)
  • Load balancing

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. However, if you disable essential cookies, some parts of the Service may not function properly.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Any analytics we perform is done using first-party cookies or privacy-respecting analytics tools.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at privacy@deployr.com
