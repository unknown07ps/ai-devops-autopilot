Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect Prometheus to Deployr?
Add Deployr as a remote_write target in your Prometheus configuration. See our documentation for the complete setup guide.
Is my data sent to external servers?
When using local LLM inference (Ollama), your data is processed entirely on your infrastructure. No data is sent externally unless you explicitly configure cloud features.
Can I control which actions are auto-approved?
Yes, you can configure auto-approval thresholds per action type. Start with manual approval for all actions and gradually enable auto-approval as you build trust.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive an email with a secure reset link valid for 24 hours.
